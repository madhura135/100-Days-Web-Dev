Madhura's Investments

Mutual Funds

A mutual fund pools money from multiple investors and invests it in stocks, bonds, or other assets. It is professionally managed and helps beginners invest without selecting individual stocks.

PPF (Public Provident Fund)

PPF is a government-backed long-term savings scheme that offers guaranteed returns and tax benefits. It has a 15-year lock-in period and is considered one of the safest investment options in India.

Fixed Deposit (FD)

A fixed deposit allows you to invest money with a bank for a fixed period at a predetermined interest rate. It provides stable returns with low risk but usually offers lower growth than equity investments.

Gold

Gold is a traditional investment that helps protect wealth during inflation and economic uncertainty. Investors can buy physical gold, digital gold, or gold ETFs instead of storing jewelry.

Stocks

Stocks represent ownership in a company and can generate wealth through price appreciation and dividends. They offer higher return potential than many other investments but also carry higher risk.
